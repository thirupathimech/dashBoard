Our Story

About AutoElite

Founded in 2012, AutoElite began with a simple mission: make buying a quality second-hand car a stress-free, transparent experience. What started as a small lot has grown into one of the region's most respected pre-owned dealerships.

We specialise in sourcing low-mileage, single-owner vehicles with full service histories. Every car is personally inspected by our certified mechanics before it reaches our showroom floor.

150-point vehicle inspection on every car
