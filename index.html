🏦 Loan Calculator

Our Loan Calculator helps you easily understand your loan payments and schedule. Here’s how it works:

How It Works

  1. 📥 Input Your Details
    • 💰 Loan Amount: Enter the total amount you want to borrow.
    • 📅 Tenure (Months): Specify how long you will take to repay the loan.
    • 📈 Interest Rate: Provide the annual interest rate for your loan.
  2. 🔢 Calculate
    • 🖱️ Click "Calculate": Our tool will instantly compute your monthly payment and the total repayable amount.
    • 📊 View Results: See the detailed breakdown of your monthly payments and the total amount you’ll repay over the entire loan period.
  3. 🗓️ Payment Schedule
    • 📅 Monthly Breakdown: Check each month’s payment details, including:
      • Principal: The portion of your payment that goes towards reducing the loan balance.
      • Interest: The portion of your payment that covers the interest on your loan.
      • Total Payment: The total amount you pay each month.
      • Remaining Balance: The remaining amount you owe after each payment.

Benefits

  • 💡 Easy to Use: Simply input your loan details and get instant results.
  • 🔍 Transparent: Understand exactly how much you’ll pay each month and overall.
  • 📈 Plan Better: Use the payment schedule to plan your finances effectively.

Example

  • 💰 Loan Amount: $10,000
  • 📅 Tenure: 12 months
  • 📈 Interest Rate: 5% per annum

Result:

  • Monthly Payment: $856.07
  • Total Repayable Amount: $10,272.84
